🔐 Privacy Policy

Smart Notes stores notes locally on your device. If you sign in with Google, notes can also sync to your own Firebase account for your use.

We do not sell your data. Your profile details and note data are only used for app login, sync, and app features.

Guest notes remain on your device. If guest lock password is forgotten and reset is used, local guest notes may be deleted.

Support links open only when you tap them. By using this app, you agree to this privacy behavior.
